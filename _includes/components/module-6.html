First comes home, then comes marriage.

Once engaged, Ryan and Carrie embark on some ambitious life planning which leaves them walking a financial tightrope.

If things go according to plan, Ryan and Carrie will collect the keys to their new home within a month of getting hitched.

As they wait for their home to be ready, they plan and save for several considerable costs, including a modest wedding and minor home renovations, before finally moving into their BTO and purchasing a car.

Throughout their time carefully researching the home buying process, Ryan and Carrie have learned that during the BTO application process, either a Whole or Term insurance policy fulfills the requirements for Home Protection Insurance, which is required by the HDB.
